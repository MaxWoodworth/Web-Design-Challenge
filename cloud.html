Cloudiness

This grahpic shows the relationship between Cloudiness(represented as a percent on the Y axis) and Latitude(represented on the X axis). There seems to be little correlation in this comparison and as we can see it is physically impossible for there to be a cloudiness % of greater than 100 or less than 0. Although it is interesting to not that there are quite a few instances of about 78% cloudiness across the globe.
